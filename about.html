Gretchen Lawson, AGNP-C

Nurse Practitioner / Provider

Gretchen Lawson is a seasoned and dedicated board-certified Adult-Gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioner with over 30 years in the healthcare industry. She has consistently demonstrated a commitment to enhancing the health and well-being of individuals through comprehensive and compassionate care. Throughout her professional journey, she has developed a passion for helping underprivileged people in our communities build healthier lifestyles.
